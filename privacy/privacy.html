Privacy Policy for DIU Notes Buddy

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

Ocean Mallik ("we," "us," or "our") operates the DIU Notes Android application and the DIU Notes Buddy web version at diunotesbuddy.live (collectively, the "Application" or "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Application or website. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the application or website.

1. Information We Collect

A. User Account Information
You have the option to use DIU Notes and diunotesbuddy.live anonymously or to create an account. If you use the app or website without logging in, we do not collect any personal identity information. If you choose to log in using third-party providers (Google or GitHub) via our authentication provider, Supabase, we collect your Name, Email Address, and Profile Picture. This information is used solely to create and manage your user profile within the service.

B. User-Generated Content (Notes and PDFs)
DIU Notes and diunotesbuddy.live allow you to create text notes and attach PDF files (or view them via the web catalog). This content is transmitted securely and stored using GitHub. In the Android app, we rely on Android’s native file picker to allow you to upload PDFs. We do not request broad read/write storage permissions to your device; you retain full control over which specific files you select and upload to the Application.

C. Automatically Collected Information & Advertising
Our Application integrates with Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob automatically collects certain device information, such as your IP address, device identifiers (like the Android Advertising ID), and usage data to serve relevant ads.

2. Third-Party Services We Use

The Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. The third-party service providers used by this Application include Supabase (for secure user authentication and session management), GitHub (as the backend storage mechanism for your notes and PDF files), and Google AdMob (for displaying advertisements within the Application). You can review the privacy policies of these third-party providers on their respective websites.

3. Device Permissions

DIU Notes and the diunotesbuddy.live web version are designed to respect your privacy and minimize permission requests. The Android app does not explicitly ask for runtime permissions for your contacts, camera, or background location. When attaching a PDF, the Application utilizes the system's built-in file picker, meaning the app only gains access to the specific file you choose to share with it. While the services require standard internet access to sync notes, handle authentication, and load advertisements, they do not require special network permission prompts from the user.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to create and manage your account securely, sync and retrieve your notes and PDF files across devices, serve advertisements to support the development of the Application, and respond to your customer service requests.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data and account information at any time. If you wish to have your account, profile data (name, email, profile picture), and stored notes (including PDFs) permanently deleted from our systems, please send an email request to oceanmallik@oceanmallik.com. Please include the email address associated with your DIU Notes account so we can locate and remove your data. We will process your deletion request promptly upon receipt.

6. Security of Your Data

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. Authentication is handled securely through Supabase, and notes are transmitted securely to GitHub. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, please contact us at the email address below so we can take steps to remove that information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at oceanmallik@oceanmallik.com.
