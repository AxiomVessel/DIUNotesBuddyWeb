DIU Notes Buddy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DIU Notes Buddy handles information when you use the Android app or visit the website. The app is designed to provide study notes, text, and images fetched from public GitHub repositories.

Overview

DIU Notes Buddy is an educational utility app. It does not require user accounts, logins, or sign-ups.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, student IDs, phone numbers, or location data.

We also do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

How We Use Information

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not use personal information for profiling, advertising, or analytics. The app only uses network access to fetch and display public study content from GitHub.

Device Permissions

The app may request local storage or media access only when you actively choose to download and save a note or file to your device. We do not access files outside of the content you choose to download.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services. Content is fetched through standard network requests to GitHub-hosted public repositories.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data retention period. Any notes or files downloaded by you remain on your device until you delete them.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to keep the app and website functioning securely, but no internet service is completely secure. Please use the app on trusted devices and networks.

Your Choices

You can choose whether to download files to your device. You can also uninstall the app at any time and delete any files you saved locally.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for students and general educational use. Because we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new Last Updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at oceanmallik@oceanmallik.com.
